## Pure Tone Audiometry

- **Frequencies tested**: 250, 500, 1000, 2000, 4000, 8000 Hz

- **Procedure**: Modified Hughson-Westlake (down 10 dB, up 5 dB)

- **Threshold**: Softest level a tone is heard 50% of the time

  - Right ear (unmasked): `O`

  - Left ear (unmasked): `X`
